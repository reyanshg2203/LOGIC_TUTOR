INDO Clausal Form Conversion Diagnostic Problems

Each conversion rule is a concept a student must hold. A small set of edit operators (swap a connective, drop a negation, negate the wrong side, drop a conjunct, skip a step, swap a reading) is applied across every rule to generate the whole space of possible misconceptions. Pick a target, and the generator finds a problem where every way of getting that one concept wrong yields an answer no other rule can imitate.
